Aethereal Architecture Landing Page Template
A cinematic, dark brutalist landing page template crafted for architecture and interior studios to showcase flagship projects and attract high-end clients.

Immersive hero with oversized typography and city-based studio intro
Sticky card stack layout for "Selected Works" case studies
Philosophy section highlighting design principles and values
Scroll-triggered GSAP animations and Lenis smooth scrolling
Full-screen footer with "LET'S TALK" project inquiry CTA and social links
Use cases

Architecture or interior design studio portfolio
Concept studio or visualization agency showcase
High-impact single-page marketing site for design-led practices
Use this template to present projects, narrative, and contact details in a refined, editorial experience that feels premium and timeless.
